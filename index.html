About Me
That's me, actually!
Hey, I'm Roksolana
Currently designing at Trinetix

I'm a Product Designer who likes turning complex workflows, edge cases, and business logic into clear product experiences. My Big 4 audit background helps me approach design with structure, logic, and attention to detail.

Recently, I've been focused on AI-powered SaaS, data-heavy B2B tools, and design systems. I also actively use Figma Make and Claude in my design process to explore product logic, test flow variations, and validate ideas faster before development.

Experience
  • Designed and shipped AI-powered product features used by 90,000+ internal users, owning the end-to-end UX process from discovery and user flows to high-fidelity design and developer handoff.
  • Built and scaled a design system using design tokens, Figma Variables, reusable components, and shared patterns to improve UI consistency across three product lines.
  • Defined design delivery standards, including file structure, naming conventions, component usage, and accessibility guidelines, improving collaboration between design and engineering teams.
  • Leveraged AI-assisted prototyping tools, including Figma Make and Claude, to support moderated and unmoderated usability testing before development, improving task completion by ~23%.
  • Designed a PWA version of the product, increasing mobile session duration by ~35% and improving accessibility across devices.
  • Conducted accessibility audits, established WCAG compliance standards, and led workshops on accessibility and upsell strategies for both the design team and broader Trinetix organization.
  • Led end-to-end UX/UI design across 6 complex B2B and B2C platforms in AI, fintech, blockchain, recruitment, and energy tech — from discovery and user flows to high-fidelity design and developer handoff.
  • Redesigned NextRep, an AI-powered outbound sales platform, improving sales team productivity by 41%, reducing churn by 24%, and increasing task completion by 33%.
  • Designed Harmoni, a recruitment SaaS platform that streamlined end-to-end hiring workflows for HR teams managing complex candidate pipelines.
  • Built Astoria Hybrid, a solar plant management dashboard for real-time performance monitoring, issue tracking, and maintenance operations across multiple locations.
  • Designed Fourcore, a blockchain investment platform with KYC flows, simplifying a highly regulated user journey without sacrificing product depth or compliance clarity.
  • Conducted UX audits, competitive analysis, and usability testing to identify bottlenecks and optimize key flows, including onboarding, lead generation, queue management, and candidate review.
  • Built and maintained scalable component libraries and design systems, improving UI consistency and speeding up developer handoff across multiple products.
  • Led UX/UI design and redesign of complex platforms and MVPs across government, AI, and aviation sectors — from wireframes and user flows to high-fidelity UI and developer handoff.
  • Redesigned Frontdesk, a large-scale queue management system used by public institutions, including staff scheduling dashboards and queue displays for waiting areas.
  • Supported Frontdesk's expansion into the Chinese market by designing and testing layouts across varied Chinese character lengths, text overflow scenarios, and multilingual interface constraints.
  • Refreshed the UX/UI for Hermetic AI, an AI-powered lead processing platform, creating a cleaner and more modern interface aligned with the product's intelligent automation capabilities.
  • Streamlined daily workflows for Mach8, an internal platform for pilots, simplifying complex operational interfaces for faster and more efficient day-to-day use.

NextRep gives sales teams an AI-powered edge — helping them automate outreach, enrich lead data, and craft smarter messages. We redesigned NextRep from the ground up, building a bold, intuitive user experience that mirrors its AI-first vision.

Every screen was reimagined to turn complex workflows into simple, data-driven decisions. Working hand-in-hand with the product team, we modernised a legacy system into a clear, focused platform — simplifying workflows, clarifying key signals, and supporting users across the full sales cycle.

key challenges
Users didn't see the value — high churn at the cancellation point.
Added a 25% retention offer at cancellation — significantly boosted retention.
Users struggled to find where to begin in a complex multi-step workflow.
Added demo mode and a step-by-step onboarding guide for instant orientation.
UX was cluttered with fragmented dashboards and noisy metrics.
Merged views and prioritised relevant data — fewer screens, clearer signal.
Crucial information was buried in modals, forcing unnecessary clicks.
Surfaced data on clean, dedicated pages — everything accessible in context.
